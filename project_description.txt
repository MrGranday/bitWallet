**bitWallet: Your Secure and Modern Bitcoin Wallet**

bitWallet is a feature-rich and secure Bitcoin wallet built with Rust, offering a seamless experience for managing your digital assets. It combines the speed and security of Rust with the power of the Bitcoin Development Kit (bdk) to provide a robust and reliable platform for all your Bitcoin transactions. Whether you're a seasoned crypto enthusiast or just starting, bitWallet offers an intuitive and powerful interface for all your needs.

Our platform provides a comprehensive suite of features designed for modern cryptocurrency users. With bitWallet, you can effortlessly send and receive Bitcoin, monitor your transaction history, and check your balance in real-time. The wallet generates unique deposit addresses for enhanced privacy and security. When you're ready to transact, our system allows you to create, sign, and broadcast Bitcoin transactions directly to the network. We've also implemented robust user authentication with password hashing to ensure your funds are always protected.

Under the hood, bitWallet is powered by a high-performance Rust backend, a `tokio`-based asynchronous runtime, and a MongoDB database for reliable data storage. The application's architecture is designed for scalability and security, with a dedicated module for blockchain interaction using the `bdk` library and an Esplora server. All functionalities are exposed through a clean and efficient API, making it easy to integrate with various frontend applications. bitWallet is the perfect blend of cutting-edge technology and user-friendly design, making it the ideal choice for managing your Bitcoin assets.
